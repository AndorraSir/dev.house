OUIFIND

Ouifind is a small yet promising real estate consultancy company in Barcelona with great potential. It belongs to Julien Gondeau, a french real estate consultant who offers to guide his customers through the whole process of finding the right flat in Barcelona.

THE BRIEF

Ouifind faced several challenges; it needed a new fresh design that made navigation through it easier in order to lead it's users to the key points. The design should also highlight and expose Julien's services in a more optimized fashion. As in recent years the number of visitors using mobile and tablet devices has been on the rise, a beautiful responsive website is no longer a luxury, but more of a necessity.

THE PROCESS

I started by doing a little research on the new design and getting some visual inpiration. Once I had a few ideas, I began drawing the basic structure in wireframes using Sketch. As this is an important step, it required some iterating between Julien and myself until the layout and structure of the site was just right.

To create the new visual concept I tweeked the old color palette, revised the imagery and iconography, and proposed different typography combinations. By definig the visual guidelines for mobile, tablet and desktop, we were then able to see the final look and feel of the site. After a few re touches here and there, we were both finally happy with how the site was looking, and decided it was time to go ahead and integrate it with Wordpress CMS.
