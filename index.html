WHAT I DO

Since 2007 I’ve worked with companies of all shapes and sizes, from multinationals to startups, turning complex problems into simple intuitive interface designs. My areas of expertise are:

DESIGN 

I love making things look good! I design beautiful interfaces that combine visual appeal and great user experience.

DEVELOPMENT 

Using a combination of HTML5 & CSS3, design, and client-side scripts and frameworks, I put together fully responsive websites that fit your needs.

VISUAL IDENTITY 

I shape a set of guidlines to establish consistency amongst the brand's visual assets, which in turn will create a reconizable brand personality.

THE PROCESS

Everyone has a way of doing things...

1.DISCOVER

I conduct a design brief to understand your needs and requirements, I listen to your concerns and ideas, and then I reserach all of this information to put together a plan of action.

2.DESIGN

Once I have an outline of the project, I start to develop a visual concept that reflects your goals and objectives, and that reinforces your online brand identity.

3.DEVELOP

This is where the magic happens... now that the solution has been clearly designed, I start to code the mockups into interactive, responsive web pages.

4.DELIVER

Once the website is complete, it goes through extensive testing before and after the launch to make ensure the quality of the product amongst multiple device sizes.
